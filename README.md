# 📊 Amazon Sales Report Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an **interactive Amazon Sales Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, designed to analyze and visualize sales performance across different regions, categories, shipping modes, and markets. The dashboard helps in identifying key trends, high-performing areas, and opportunities for business growth.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- **Dataset** – Amazon Sales Dataset (includes sales, profit, shipping, and geographic data)  

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 178K  
- **Total Sales:** 12.64M  
- **Total Profit:** 1.47M  
- **Total Shipping Cost:** 1.35M  

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

---

## 📦 Dashboard Insights

### 🚚 Sales by Ship Mode
- **Standard Class** generates the highest sales  
- **Second Class** and **First Class** contribute moderately  
- **Same Day delivery** has the lowest sales  

💡 **Insight:** Customers prefer economical shipping options over faster delivery.

---

### 🌍 Sales by Country
**Top contributing countries:**
- United States – 2.30M  
- Australia – 0.93M  
- France – 0.86M  
- China – 0.70M  
- Germany – 0.63M  

💡 **Insight:** The United States is the leading country in sales.

---

### 🏙️ Sales by City
**Top-performing cities:**
- New York City  
- Los Angeles  
- Manila  
- Seattle  
- San Francisco  

💡 **Insight:** Sales are heavily concentrated in major urban areas.

---

### 🗂️ Sales by Category
- **Technology:** 4.74M (Highest)  
- **Furniture:** 4.11M  
- **Office Supplies:** 3.79M  

💡 **Insight:** Technology products are the primary revenue drivers.

---

### 🌐 Sales by Market
- **APAC:** 3.59M (Largest share)  
- **EU:** 2.94M  
- **US:** 2.30M  
- **LATAM:** 2.16M  
- Others: Smaller contributions  

💡 **Insight:** APAC region leads overall market performance.

---

## 🔍 Features
- Interactive filters for **Category** and **Market**  
- Dynamic visualizations for deeper analysis  
- Easy-to-understand layout for business users
